Payments by library

Payments taken in last year, grouped by library paid and payment type. This shows the amount being taken by each library in payments, but also by the different payment types (e.g. CASH). It will also show, from FORGIVEN and CANCELLED payments, income that isn't been taken for these reasons.

Column Description
Authority The authority that took the payment
Library The library that took the payment
Payment Type The payment type used e.g. 'Cash'. Remember that 'Cancelled' and 'Forgiven' are also payment types
Number of payments The number of payments that make up this grouping
Total paid The total amount paid in this grouping
