Bills by reason and authority

Bills incurred in last year, grouped by bill reason and billing authority. This data will highlight obvious differences between authorities such as where some have Hold charges, and others don't. But also can be used to see a breakdown of the type of bills that generate income for each authority.

Column Description
Authority The authority that the bill is assigned to
Reason The reason for the bill e.g. Overdue
Number of bills The number of bills that make up this grouping
Total billed The total amount billed in this grouping
