Payments by authority, item type, and month

Payments taken in last year, grouped by authority paid, item type, and month the payment was taken. This data can be useful in seeing what item types are causing payments to be taken. It also includes item authority as well as payment authority so can be used for calculating interlending charges between authorities.

Column Description
Payment Authority The authority that took the payment
Payment Month The month that payment was taken
Item Authority The authority that owns the item that the bill was related to
Item Type The type of item that the bill was related to.
Reason The reason for the bill e.g. Overdue
Payment Type The payment type used e.g. 'Cash'. Remember that 'Cancelled' and 'Forgiven' are also payment types
Number of payments The number of payments that make up this grouping
Total paid The total amount paid in this grouping
