Issues by authority

Number of issues per month for the previous 6 months, separated by issuing authority. An issue is the lending of a physical item to a borrower. Authority is the library authority the physical item was loaned at. Month is the month of the activity. Issues is a count of the number of issues for that month and library.

Issues by library

Number of issues for the previous 6 months, separated by issuing library. Libraries with less than 50,000 annual issues are not displayed in the graph but are in the data table below.

Issues by ward

Number of issues for the previous 6 months, separated by the ward of the user's home address. Darker shading represents more issues. Click on a Ward to see further details.

Holds by authority

Holds placed per month for the previous 6 months, separated by authority.
